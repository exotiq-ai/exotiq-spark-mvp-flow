## 🏗️ Core Modules

### 1. Dashboard (Command Center)
**Purpose**: Real-time overview of entire fleet operation

**Key Elements to Demo**:
- Revenue analytics cards (Today, Week, Month, Year)
- Fleet utilization percentage with visual indicator
- AI Insight Banner (proactive recommendations)
- Quick action buttons (New Booking, Add Vehicle, Record Payment)
- Fleet Status & Schedule widget (collapsible)
- "24/7 AI Monitoring" badge in header

**Demo Highlight**: The dashboard aggregates everything - click any metric to drill down.

---

### 2. Book (Reservations & Calendar)
**Purpose**: Visual booking management with calendar views

**Key Elements to Demo**:
- Calendar view with booking blocks by vehicle
- Color-coded booking status (confirmed, pending, completed)
- Click-to-create booking on empty dates
- Booking details panel with customer info
- AI-suggested pricing on new bookings

**Demo Highlight**: Create a booking and show how AI suggests optimal pricing based on demand.

---

### 3. MotorIQ (Pricing Intelligence)
**Purpose**: AI-powered dynamic pricing and fleet analytics

**Key Elements to Demo**:
- **Overview Tab**: Fleet performance scatter plot, vehicle cards with profit indicators
- **Calendar Tab**: Pricing heatmap by day, visual revenue intensity
- **Dynamic Pricing Tab**: AI recommendations with confidence scores
- **Demand Forecast Tab**: Event-aware pricing (concerts, sports, holidays)
- **Quick Price Editor**: Click any vehicle card → instant rate editing dialog

**Demo Highlight**: Click a vehicle → Quick Price Editor opens → Apply AI recommendation with one click.

**AI Recommendation Card** (appears at top when available):
```
💡 Pricing Opportunity: Lamborghini Huracán
   Current: $1,200/day | Suggested: $1,450/day (+20.8%)
   Reason: Coachella weekend demand spike
   [Apply Rate] [Dismiss]
```

---

### 4. Pulse (Real-time Operations)
**Purpose**: Live operational monitoring and alerts

**Key Elements to Demo**:
- **Today's Snapshot**: Pickups, returns, vehicles out
- **Vehicles Out Now**: Live map pins, customer info
- **Attention Required**: Overdue returns, late payments, maintenance due
- **Fleet Status Donut**: Visual breakdown of fleet state
- **Next 4 Hours**: Upcoming actions timeline
- **Driver Telematics**: Speed, location, fuel level (if GPS enabled)

**Demo Highlight**: Show the "Attention Required" section - this is where operators spend most of their time.

- System health indicators

- Integration settings
- Activity logs

**Demo Highlight**: Show the "FleetCopilot™ is monitoring your fleet 24/7" message.

### 7. CRM (Customer Management)

### B. Command Palette (Cmd+K)
**What It Is**: Instant navigation and search across entire platform

**Demo**:
1. Press Cmd+K (or Ctrl+K on Windows)
2. Type "Ferrari" → Shows matching vehicles
3. Type "booking" → Shows recent bookings
4. Type "settings" → Navigate to settings

**Talking Point**: "Power users can navigate the entire platform without touching the mouse."

---

### C. Click-to-Edit Pricing (MotorIQ)
**What It Is**: Inline editing of vehicle rates without leaving the overview

**Demo Flow**:
1. Go to MotorIQ → Overview tab
2. Click any vehicle card
3. Quick Price Editor dialog opens
4. Show current rate, AI suggestion, date overrides
5. Apply new rate → Shows success toast

**Talking Point**: "Change prices in seconds, not minutes. No spreadsheets, no back-and-forth."

---

### D. Pricing Calendar Heatmap
**What It Is**: Visual representation of bookings and revenue by day

**Demo**:
1. Go to MotorIQ → Calendar tab
2. Show color intensity = booking density
3. Click a day → See vehicle-level detail
4. Point out patterns (weekends darker, events visible)

**Talking Point**: "See your revenue patterns at a glance. Spot opportunities instantly."

---

### E. AI-Powered Booking Suggestions
**What It Is**: When creating a new booking, AI suggests optimal pricing

**Demo Flow**:
1. Click "New Booking" from dashboard
2. Select vehicle and dates
3. AI pricing section shows:
   - Suggested rate vs. default rate
   - Reason (demand factors, events)
   - One-click apply

**Talking Point**: "Never leave money on the table. AI ensures optimal pricing on every booking."

---

### F. Team Collaboration
**What It Is**: Share specific issues with teammates via deep links

**Demo**:
1. Go to Vault → Damage Claims
2. Click a claim
3. Click "Share with Team"
4. Copy link → Paste in chat
5. Recipient goes directly to that claim

**Talking Point**: "Collaboration is built-in. Share any issue with one click."

## 🎨 Design & UX Highlights

### Visual Identity
- **Theme**: Dark mode optimized, luxury automotive aesthetic
- **Accent Colors**: Gulf racing heritage (teal/orange accents)
- **Typography**: Clean, modern, high-contrast
- **Cards**: Subtle borders, hover effects, depth via shadows

### UX Excellence
- **Mobile Responsive**: Full functionality on tablet/phone
- **Touch Targets**: 44px minimum (iOS guideline compliant)
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Cmd+K (search), Cmd+N (new), Cmd+Shift+R (Rari)
- **Loading States**: Skeleton loaders, not spinners
- **Empty States**: Beautiful illustrations, not error messages
- **Dialogs**: Scroll-safe with sticky footers (save button always visible)

## ⚠️ Demo Tips & Troubleshooting

### What to Show
- ✅ Dashboard with real demo data populated
- ✅ Rari responding to voice commands
- ✅ Click-through flows (booking, pricing)
- ✅ Mobile responsive views
- ✅ AI recommendations appearing

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Empty states (unless demoing empty state design)
- ❌ Error messages
- ❌ Slow loading (pause recording during loads)
- ❌ Half-completed forms

### Preparation Checklist
- [ ] Demo account logged in
- [ ] Sample data populated (vehicles, bookings, customers)
- [ ] Browser at 100% zoom
- [ ] Dark mode enabled
- [ ] Microphone working for Rari demo
- [ ] Notifications cleared
