# Command Center — Trainer Checklist

One-page pre-flight for the live training. Run through this 10 minutes before you go live.

Pair with `LIVE_TRAINING.md` (the script itself).

---

## ✈️ Pre-flight (10 min before)

- [ ] Open browser in **incognito / private window** (clean state, no stale cache).
- [ ] Sign in with the **demo account** (not a real tenant).
- [ ] Set browser zoom to **100%** (screen-share readability).
- [ ] **Silence** notifications: OS Do Not Disturb + Slack/email closed.
- [ ] Test **Rari mic permission** — grant access, say "Hi Rari," confirm response.
- [ ] Screen-share at **1080p or higher**. Hide bookmarks bar.
- [ ] Dark mode: pick one and stick with it for the whole session.
- [ ] Have `LIVE_TRAINING.md` open on a **second screen** as your teleprompter.

---

## 📦 Data Readiness (the demo needs live data)

- [ ] At least **1 vehicle** in the fleet with a hero photo.
- [ ] At least **1 active/upcoming booking** to click into.
- [ ] At least **1 logged expense** so Margin renders non-zero.
- [ ] At least **1 partner** on the roster (for payouts demo).
- [ ] A **sample photo** on your desktop ready to drag into the vehicle uploader.
- [ ] Weekly intelligence digest has been generated at least once.

## ⚠️ Common Pitfalls (watch for these live)

- **⌘K vs browser find:** if browser find opens instead, you missed focus — click into the app first.
- **Rari mic silent:** browser may have blocked mic permission — check the URL bar padlock.
- **Demo vs live account:** if you see real customer data, stop — you're in the wrong tenant.
- **Dark mode toggle mid-session:** don't. It re-flows the layout and distracts.
- **Onboarding tour firing:** clear `exotiq-onboarding-completed` in localStorage **before** the call, not during.
- **Confetti at the wrong moment:** it's charming, not a bug. Roll with it.
