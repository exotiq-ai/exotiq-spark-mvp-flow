## 1. Welcome (0:30)

**What it is:** Command Center is the operator cockpit for your exotic fleet — bookings, fleet, margin, partners, and AI insights in one place.

**Who it's for:** Owners, fleet managers, and front-desk staff who need to move fast without opening five tabs.

> **Trainer says:** *"Everything you do daily lives behind one keystroke or one click. By the end of this session you'll know the five tools that cover 90% of your day."*

---

## 2. The 5-Tool Starter Kit (10:00)

### 2.1 Cmd+K — Global Search (2 min)

- Press **⌘K** (Mac) or **Ctrl+K** (Windows) from anywhere.
- Type a vehicle name, booking ref, partner, or page — hit Enter.
- Back button works. URL persists. No mouse required.

> **Trainer says:** *"If you only remember one shortcut, remember this one."*

### 2.2 Rari — Your AI Copilot (2 min)

- Press **⌘O** to toggle Rari.
- Ask in plain English: *"What's my utilization this week?"*, *"Which vehicles are unprofitable?"*, *"Who owes me a deposit refund?"*
- Rari returns insights **and** clickable action items that deep-link into the app.

> **Trainer says:** *"Rari knows your data. Ask like you'd ask a smart analyst."*

### 2.3 Pulse / Dashboard Hero Rail (2 min)

- Landing view. Hero KPI rail shows **what changed today**: revenue, utilization, deltas, sparklines.
- Click any KPI delta chip → drills into the source data.
- "Needs Attention" surfaces items that require action *today*.

> **Trainer says:** *"Pulse is your morning coffee. 30 seconds and you know if the day needs you."*

### 2.4 Book — The Booking Engine (2 min)

- Create a booking inline — no wizard.
- Live pricing updates as you change dates, vehicle, or tier (hourly vs multiday).
- Calendar view: **conflict rings** flash red on overlapping bookings.
- Global search finds any booking by reference (`BK-01001`), guest, or vehicle.

> **Trainer says:** *"One screen, live math, no surprises."*

### 2.5 Margin — Where the Money Truth Lives (2 min)

- Top: **Needs Attention** strip — pending expenses, payouts, stale deposits. Click a chip to jump to that tab.
- Hero rail: **Operator Net → Margin % → Gross Booked** with deltas vs prior period.
- Per-Vehicle P&L: sticky header, totals footer, margin % color-coded (≥30% green, ≥10% amber, <10% red).
- Emphasized column: **Operator Net** — the number that pays the bills.

> **Trainer says:** *"Content is here. If a number looks off, click the chip — it takes you to what to fix."*

## 3. Tips & Tricks (3:00)

### Keyboard shortcuts (memorize these five)
- **⌘K** — global search
- **⌘O** — toggle Rari
- **⌘/** — show all keyboard shortcuts
- **⌘1–5** — jump to MotorIQ / Pulse / Book / Vault / Core
- **Esc** — close any dialog or overlay

### Power moves
- **Click a KPI delta** → drills to source data. Every headline number is a link.
- **Margin action chips** → each chip navigates to the exact tab with the work to do.
- **Notifications center** → every notification deep-links to the exact record. Never hunt.
- **Banner white-labeling** → hover the dashboard banner, upload your tenant logo/artwork. Instant demo-ready.
- **Command Palette from a mobile browser** → still works. Touch the search icon.

## 4. Common Workflows (Cheat Sheet — 1:00)

### 4.1 Add a vehicle → hero photo → publish
1. **⌘K → "new vehicle"**
2. Fill essentials, save.
3. Upload photos — the **first photo becomes the hero shot** automatically.
4. Toggle to **Available**. It's now live in the booking engine.

### 4.2 Create a booking → send agreement → check in
1. **⌘K → "new booking"** (or Book module → New).
2. Pick vehicle + dates. Watch pricing update live.
3. Send digital agreement (8 renter acknowledgements auto-attached).
4. On arrival: **Check-In** wizard → photos → signatures → keys handed over.

### 4.3 Log an expense → see it in Margin
1. Margin → **Review** tab → Add Expense.
2. Assign to vehicle + category.
3. Reload Margin — **Operator Net** and per-vehicle P&L update immediately.

### 4.4 Review weekly digest → act on top item
1. Open weekly intelligence digest (email or in-app).
2. Top insight has an action link — click it.
3. You land on the exact record. Take action. Done.
