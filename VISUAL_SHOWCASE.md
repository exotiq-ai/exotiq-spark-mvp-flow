## 🏆 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

1. **Clarity First:** Essential info upfront, details on demand
2. **Delight Moments:** Micro-interactions make work enjoyable
3. **Keyboard-First:** Power users can navigate without mouse
4. **Automotive Heritage:** Gulf livery, racing stripes, tachometers
5. **Progressive Disclosure:** Reduce cognitive load
6. **Beautiful Empty States:** Guide users, never confuse
7. **Smooth Animations:** 60fps, respects reduced motion
8. **Brand Consistency:** Gulf Blue + Performance Orange everywhere
